DID YOU KNOW THAT A WASTE MANAGEMENT PLAN IS REQUIRED FOR YOUR DEVELOPMENT APPLICATION?

For any development application, the waste management plan is required by all council in Australia. The plan outline how you intend to manage the waste generated during demolition and contruction activities, as well as the ongoing use of the project.
